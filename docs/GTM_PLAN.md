# Roomora — Go-To-Market Plan

> Owner: GTM (orchestrator). Status: v1 strategy, approved direction. Last updated: 2026-06-02.
> Companion docs: `HANDOFF.md` (product state), `DECISIONS.md`, `PROJECT_MEMORY.md`.

## 1. The bet (executive summary)

Roomora wins on two advantages no competitor has, and the entire launch is built on them:

1. **Spatial fidelity as the hero promise.** Every competitor (homedesigns.ai, reimaginehome.ai, InteriorAI) buries "keeps your real room" as a feature footnote, and their reviews still complain the tools invent windows and reinvent rooms. We make that complaint our headline.
2. **Native bilingual EN + 简体中文.** No major competitor ships a Chinese UX. This opens RedNote (小红书), a massive home/furnishing community where no competitor is playing. First-mover land grab, not a crowded fight.

**Primary channel: RedNote (Chinese-first).** **Secondary: Reddit (value-first organic + launch spikes).** **Always-on support: founder-led short-form video (TikTok/Reels) + Pinterest SEO.** One hero asset feeds all of them.

**Positioning line (EN):** *"Restyle your real room. Same walls, same windows. Still yours."*
**Positioning line (中文):** *"AI 重新设计你真实的家，墙、窗、门都不乱改，还是你那间屋。"*

## 2. Positioning & messaging

| Element | Roomora | Why it beats the field |
|---|---|---|
| Core promise | Restyles YOUR actual room, structure preserved | Competitors hallucinate windows/doors; #1 user complaint |
| Control | Natural-language refine ("add a rug", "warmer light") | Demonstrable on camera; rivals can't show this with stock rooms |
| Audience | Renters + first-time furnishers, Paris/Europe + Chinese-speaking | Incumbents skew US real-estate agents |
| Pricing trust | 3 free designs, no card, no watermark, one-click cancel | homedesigns' billing complaints are our moral high ground |
| Language | Native EN + 简体中文 | Zero competitor offers Chinese UX |

**Message hierarchy (all copy leads in this order):** (1) it's still *your* room, (2) upload to result in seconds, (3) refine in plain language, (4) free to try, no card.

**Tone:** warm and aspirational, not aggressive. Avoid InteriorAI's "fire your interior designer" framing; it alienates the helpful, design-curious renter we want.

## 3. Audience & channel strategy

### Primary — RedNote (小红书), Chinese-first

The differentiated moat. Demand is proven (租房改造 / rental-makeover exceeds 110K notes; AI-home accounts have grown to 20K+ followers and 150K likes in two months). Audience skews ~72% female, ~80% aged 18-35, split between aspirational design (精致生活) and budget (性价比): exactly our renter.

The constraint is the walled garden. As of Jan 2025, the official DM tool (私信通) is the only compliant way to move users off-platform. External links, WeChat/phone numbers, QR codes, even homophones in notes/comments/DMs trigger 7-30 day muting or bans.

**Conversion playbook:**
- Open a **企业专业号** (business account, ~¥600/yr) to unlock compliant 私信通 lead cards. Non-negotiable.
- Funnel: note → DM entry on the note → keyword auto-reply delivers a compliant social card (no WeChat/phone text).
- Evaluate a **薯店** (in-platform shop) to sell credit packs without users ever leaving (sidesteps link bans). Verify cross-border merchant eligibility for an EU entity before committing.

### Secondary — Reddit (value-first + launch spikes)

Not a volume engine. Use it for trust-building, early feedback, and launch amplification. Overt promo gets banned; run the 95/5 rule (≈19 helpful actions per promo) and disclose "I built this" transparently.
- **r/DesignMyRoom (~610K):** the best fit. Reply to "design my room" posts with a free Roomora redesign delivered in-comment; link only if asked. This *is* fidelity proof, on real strangers' rooms.
- **r/SideProject, r/IMadeThis:** "I built this" launch posts welcomed.
- **r/InteriorDesign (1M+):** strict, participate-only, no links.
- **Launch week:** Product Hunt (Tue-Thu, 12:01am PT) + Show HN (early week, ~9am ET), same week, reply to every comment.

### Always-on support — short-form video + Pinterest

- **TikTok-first, cross-posted to Reels.** Founder-led daily posting, free. "Rental makeover" already has ~47.5M posts. Post **French for Paris, English on a parallel account**, never mixed (dilutes search relevance).
- **Pinterest** as the evergreen SEO engine: interior design is its home turf, pins live months/years, ~3x the referral traffic of other social. 2:3 vertical pins, keyword-rich titles, Rich Pins, each linking to a landing page.

## 4. The content engine + the flywheel

**One asset, cut everywhere:** a 10-20s before/after reveal of a *real* room, with the window/door visibly surviving the restyle. The draggable slider already in the app is the same creative. Works as a TikTok video, Reels clip, RedNote carousel, Pinterest pin, and Reddit comment. Build once, repurpose to every channel.

**The flywheel — "Drop your room 👇":** Invite the audience to comment a photo of their actual room. Restyle it and post the reveal as a duet/stitch (TikTok/Reels) or reply note (RedNote). Turns the product into an infinite, free, personalized content machine, and every reveal is authentic third-party proof of fidelity. Competitors using stock rooms cannot run this on camera. It compounds: more comments → more content → more comments.

## 5. Materials / creative kit (priority order)

1. **Fidelity-proof before/after reveal** (video + slider) — hero creative for every channel.
2. **Fast mobile landing page, ONE CTA** ("design your room free") — not the homepage, not Linktree, UTM-tagged. TikTok users won't wait.
3. **Public `/share/[id]` pages** — every generated design becomes a shareable, embeddable before/after. The product feature that unlocks all organic sharing. (Already on the roadmap as the "main RedNote growth lever.")
4. **Bilingual programmatic SEO style pages** — `/styles/japandi-living-room`, `/zh/风格/日式客厅`, `/ai-interior-design-paris-apartment`. Cheap, compounding traffic.
5. **RedNote 企业号** + 私信通 funnel configured.
6. **Trust/pricing page** quietly contrasting clean billing (one-click cancel, no watermark on free) against incumbents' complaints.

## 6. The 30/60/90-day plan

### Days 1-30 — Foundations + seed
- **Product (prerequisite):** ship `/share/[id]` public pages + fast single-CTA landing page. Without these, nothing converts.
- **RedNote:** register 企业专业号 (~¥600), configure 私信通 auto-reply + lead card, set up 薯店 if eligible. Build a 10-post bank of real before/afters (own rooms + beta users). Begin posting 1×/day; reply to every comment within an hour (engagement is the ranking lever).
- **Reddit:** age accounts 30+ days, daily helpful comments on r/DesignMyRoom (no links yet), start a Pinterest board of SEO-optimized before/after pins.
- **Video:** founder posts 1-2 short videos/day (before/after reveal, "I tried the viral AI room app", refine-loop demo), cross-posted to Reels. Parallel FR + EN accounts.

### Days 31-60 — Amplify
- **RedNote:** seed **20-30 KOC posts** in the home niche at ~¥500/post (5K-10K-follower 腰部素人) rather than one expensive KOL. Launch a UGC challenge ("晒出你的 AI 改造前后"). Use 蒲公英 for compliant sponsored disclosure.
- **Reddit:** post 5-8 free "I redesigned your room" before/afters; one transparent build post in r/SideProject + r/IMadeThis; seed Paris FB housing groups (bilingual).
- **Video:** recruit 30-50 nano déco creators (FR + EN), ship free credits + unique referral/affiliate codes + a 1-page brief. Launch the "Drop your room" duet/stitch campaign.

### Days 61-90 — Launch spike + optimize
- **Product Hunt + Show HN** in the same week (product polished, supporter list lined up).
- Turn the best UGC into paid retargeting creative (small budget).
- Double down on whichever format/channel shows the lowest CAC; cut the rest.

## 8. Metrics — what "done" looks like

- **North star:** free-design completions per week (the "wow" moment), then free→paid credit-pack conversion.
- **Channel KPIs:** RedNote follower growth + 私信 leads/week; TikTok/Reels watch-through + link-in-bio clicks (UTM); Pinterest outbound clicks; Reddit free-redesign threads → signups.
- **Acquisition health:** CAC by channel, free→paid conversion %, credit-pack repurchase rate.
- **90-day success bar:** a working organic engine producing before/after content daily, a measurable RedNote following with compliant lead flow, and a first cohort of paying users acquired at a CAC below the credit-pack price.

## 9. Risks & landmines

- **Fidelity must hold on camera.** If output drifts from the real room, the whole differentiator backfires publicly. Only post clips where the match is genuinely tight.
- **RedNote bans:** any WeChat/phone/QR/link/homophone in notes, comments, or DMs → muting/ban. Route everything through 私信通.
- **Gifting-only = no posts.** Pair every creator gift with a written deliverable or a small fee.
- **Reddit:** cross-posting the same promo or mass DMs triggers shadowbans. Stay value-first, never spammy.
- **Slow landing page kills short-form traffic.** Mobile speed is non-negotiable.
- **Free-credit abuse:** cap free designs, watch unit economics, monitor CAC.

## 10. Dependencies (product unlocks GTM)

Most of this organic strategy needs two product builds before it can run:
