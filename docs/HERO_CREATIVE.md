# Roomora Hero Creative Brief

**Product:** Roomora, a mobile-first bilingual (English + 简体中文) web app. Upload a photo of your real room, AI restyles it into a fully furnished interior while keeping the room recognizably yours (same walls, windows, doors, proportions). Refine with natural language. Credit-based, 3 free designs, no signup to try.

**Live:** https://roomora-gamma.vercel.app

**The wedge:** TRUSTWORTHY SPATIAL FIDELITY. Competitors (homedesigns.ai, reimaginehome, InteriorAI) generate generic stock rooms and hallucinate windows and doors. Roomora restyles YOUR room. The hero video must prove this on camera by showing the same window survive the restyle, something stock-room tools physically cannot demonstrate.

**Core line:** "Restyle your real room. Same walls, same windows. Still yours."

**Target:** Renters and first-time furnishers in Paris, France, Europe, plus Chinese-speaking renters (RedNote / 小红书 audience). Lead channel RedNote (Chinese-first), TikTok / Reels and Reddit secondary.

---

## 1. The Hero Asset Concept

A 10 to 20 second vertical before / after reveal that proves fidelity, not fantasy.

The video opens on a real, slightly messy room (the kind anyone actually lives in). We see the room get uploaded into Roomora. The restyle reveals a fully furnished, designed version of the SAME space. The single most important moment: a callout circles the window (or a door) and shows it is unchanged across the before and after. That is the proof. The room got beautiful, but it stayed the user's actual room. We close on the app interface and a clear call to try it free.

The whole piece is built around one promise the competition cannot make on camera: this is your room, not a stock photo.

## 5. The "Drop Your Room 👇" Flywheel Format

A repeatable UGC engine. Followers comment a photo of their real room and we restyle it on camera. Every restyle is a new post, so the audience supplies the content and the proof is always a stranger's real room (not a curated demo).

**Why it works:** it turns the fidelity wedge into a public dare. We are restyling rooms we did not pre-select, which is exactly what stock-room competitors cannot do.

**Reusable script template (15 to 20s):**

| Beat | What happens | On-screen text |
|---|---|---|
| Hook (0 to 2s) | Show the follower's submitted room photo (with permission). | "You dropped this room 👇 let's restyle it." |
| Setup (2 to 5s) | Upload their photo into Roomora on screen. | "Real photo. Watch what stays the same." |
| Reveal (5 to 9s) | Crossfade to the restyle, same angle. | "Same room. New look." |
| Proof (9 to 13s) | Circle the window / door, before vs after. | "Window: didn't move ✅" |
| Refine (13 to 16s) | Apply one comment request (e.g. "they asked for warmer light"). | "[follower] asked for [request]. Done." |
| CTA (16 to 20s) | Invite the next batch. | "Drop YOUR room in the comments 👇" |

Rules: only use submitted photos with explicit permission, never fabricate the requester, use "[follower]" and "[request]" placeholders until real comments come in. Pin a comment inviting submissions on every flywheel post.

## 6. Production Notes (founder, phone-shot)

The founder supplies 2 to 3 real room photos plus their own before / after generations from the live app. Everything here can be filmed on a phone.

**What to capture:**
- Real room photos: shoot 2 to 3 genuinely lived-in rooms. Slightly messy is good (unmade bed, bare rental walls, moving boxes). Authenticity is the hook.
- Framing: lock the camera angle. The before photo and the after restyle must be the same composition so the window / door fidelity reads instantly. Include at least one clear window or door in frame, that is the proof element.
- Lighting: natural daylight, shoot near the window. Avoid heavy color casts so the before and after compare cleanly.
- Format: vertical 9:16, full screen, captions burned in (most viewers watch muted).
- Length: 12 to 16s for the hero, up to 20s for flywheel posts.
- Voiceover vs text-only: lead with text-only + trending audio for reach on TikTok / Reels and RedNote. Layer a short [founder voiceover] on the proof moment when a human voice adds trust. Keep both versions so you can A/B.
- Trending audio: pick a currently trending sound with a clear beat drop and align the reveal frame to the drop. On RedNote use a popular 改造 / 家居 sound. Swap audio per platform, do not reuse the same track everywhere.
- Captions: on, large, high contrast, positioned clear of platform UI (avoid bottom-right).

**Simple phone shot list:**
1. Static clip of the real messy room, 3 to 4s, locked angle, window in frame.
2. Screen recording of uploading that exact photo into Roomora and tapping Design.
3. Screen recording of the reveal (the finished restyle), same angle as shot 1.
4. Zoom / screenshot pair of just the window before and after for the proof callout (add the circle in edit).
5. Screen recording of typing one natural-language refine ("add a rug", "warmer light") and the update.
6. Clean app screen showing "3 free designs, no signup" for the end card.

Edit: assemble in CapCut or similar, add the circle / arrow callout on the window in step 4, burn captions, sync the reveal to the audio drop.
